Graphics and Icons

  • With our imagery and icons, we strived for designs that were accessible and inclusive to everyone who used our app. We wanted to use simple icons that were universally known and understood.

  • We chose Iconduck to help use create a clean and organized Navbar for our users!

  • Here are some of the icons found in our app! We believe in both simple and playful designs that engage the user!

  • We also believe that even the subtlest touches can improve our users experience. Take a look at our various design elements scattered through our app!

  • We also made sure to include a dark mode for those who may struggle with our original colour palette.

  • We chose the imagery and icons in our app due to both their inclusivity and accessibility. The icons are simple and understandable for our users, after all, expenses and banking are already difficult as is. We wanted something all users would be able to navigate and use with ease to ensure security and trust in our app. We made sure our icons and image sizing were just right for everyone. We also did various tests with our colours to make sure the contrast was right for all users.

  • We chose to keep our app simple, clean, and calming to meet the needs and preferences of our users. Privacy, complexity, and a lack of accessibility are all problems many people face with expense apps, and we aimed to eliminate these issues. Our clean designs, stable colours and straightforward imagery all foster a welcoming and inclusive user experience.
