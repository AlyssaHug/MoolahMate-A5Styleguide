Typography

Quicksand

For our display font, we chose quicksand. This font gives a readable yet not too professional look for our display fonts since we want our expense app to follow a cute yet simple.

Libre Franklin

We wanted a clean and readable font to ensure users wouldn't be confused or lost when using the app. We put simplicity at the forefront as we chose this font.
