Typography

Quicksand

We chose Quicksand for our headers because it looks modern and friendly. Since our app focuses on keeping track of users expenses but also has a cute cow mascot and fun rewards, we wanted a font that feels approachable. Quicksand's rounded style makes tracking money feel less serious and more lighthearted.
The image on the left shows some examples of how we use Quicksand for our headers.
